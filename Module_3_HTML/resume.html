My Experience

Stock Supervisor at Vans

2018-2020

As the stock supervisor, I supported the store Manager with the execution of the daily business, customer engagement, store operations, talent supervision, and merchandising standards in the store. Being a key member of the store leadership team I oversaw and supervised shipment deliveries and maintained replenishment of stock levels on the sales floor.

  • Maintained all processes and records involved in shipping/receiving for the store.
  • Processed all store shipment in a quick and efficient manner.
  • Maximized store sales through assisting customers using (S.K.A.T.E) while upselling and increasing the KPI (Key Performance Indicators) of the store.
  • Managed the most efficient stockroom layout. Clearly labeled all boxes, stocked all items by size and categories and maintained stockroom efficiency.
  • Partnered with local charity organizations to donate inventory to those in need.
  • Consistently demonstrated clear communication, training, and coaching with management and associates to foster a strong team.
  • Provided direct coaching, feedback, training and supervision of 10 associates.
  • Achieved store sales goals and monitored sales progress & results against key targets for overall store success.

Key Holder at Payless Shoesource

2016-2019

Oversaw the work of retail staff in a store environment. In charge of monitoring employee activity, coordinating the shipping and receiving of merchandise, solving problems at a day-to-day level and assisting customers.

  • Maintained knowledge of current sales and promotions, policies regarding payment and exchanges and security practices.
  • Completed all cleaning, stocking and organizing tasks in the assigned sales area.
  • Assisted 50+ customers per day and praised by management for excellent service.
  • Built long-term customer relationships and advised customers on purchases and promotions.
  • Handled all customer relations issues in a gracious manner and in accordance with company policies.
  • Trained associates on procedures, customer service and sales techniques.
  • Coordinated the implementation of online orders for the ship from the store program.
